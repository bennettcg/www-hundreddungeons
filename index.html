Hundred Dungeons is a sword-and-sorcery hack of 5E Dungeons & Dragons that brings exploration and social interaction to the fore

A little bit old school, little bit new school, and all about supporting the creativity of the humans at the table

Take on the role of audacious fantasy figures, venture into hidden places, and draw forth awe-inspiring magic

Hundred Dungeons was created from the bones of other popular d20 fantasy games with the goals of remaining familiar to players who like those games, preserving a place for “low” or “anchored” fantasy, and keeping rules direct and easy to apply at the table
